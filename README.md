#### Compile

Using the GNU Compiler Collection (GCC).

```bash
$ gcc -o selection_sort sort.c
```

#### Execute

```bash
$ ./selection_sort brad 15 paul 23 alan 23 roy 20 diane 6 franck 10 robert 6 peter 20 john 11 albert 14 yan 15
diane:  6
robert: 6
franck: 10
john:   11
albert: 14
brad:   15
yan:    15
peter:  20
roy:    20
alan:   23
paul:   23
```
